The Team

“ There is more power in togetherness than division.~ Emanuel Cleaver”

Hand in hand, they dance with art, under the light of the moon. The magical footsteps on the stage are the hard work of our choreographers. They are the ones who feel the song, dance on the beat and inspire others to dance.
Dance is not just an art. It is a communication done based entirely on the body. And this year our choreographers have put in their effort for a marvelous show on stage. They are the ones who have felt the beat of the song and have painted the canvas with their steps.
Our Karunyans have teamed up this year and spent days to put up a breathtaking performance on stage. Let’s wait ardently for their performances in this year’s Mega Play.
