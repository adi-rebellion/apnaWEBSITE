About
The Team

“My passion is motivating
people to get results”

This is the team that works day in and day out yet remains unnoticed by everyone, for their work lies backstage. The stage management team has to coordinate and organize the stage that fits in a way every aspect and plan of the other teams.
The stage management team is the magical glue that binds all the teams together and makes sure that the event is coordinated out efficiently.
They plan, prepare and iterate until everything is set to perfection.
The stakes are high and that leaves this team with no scope for error or the slightest mistake possible, thus they work tirelessly to ensure that the stage would be perfect on the day of the event.
