The Team

“ There is more power in togetherness than division.~ Emanuel Cleaver”

This is the team that connects the whole college with the event. The Megaplay Buzz and Promotions team comprises of content writers, editors, photographers, videographers and social media influencers that all work in unison to assist in making this vision of Megaplay ‘18 a reality.
Different means of publicity are used which include posters, videos, photos, and other online content about the Megaplay.
One of the latest achievement of this group is the launch of the official website of Megaplay, which has been done for the first time ever. It keeps you updated throughout the preparation and the happening of the event.
